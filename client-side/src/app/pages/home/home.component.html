Who Are We?

We are four students who want to help the world read

What Do We Do?

We are submitting the Book Barter site as part of the Atlas Hackathon. We want a way for people to connect to other people so that people can easily trade for books that they wish to read.

How Does It Work?


You add books up for trade to a collection with an ISBN number, the title, and author.

You search for books you want (near you) and join trade queues on other people's books.

People can also see your books and ask you to trade for them with their own books.
